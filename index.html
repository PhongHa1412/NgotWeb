About Ngọt

Vũ Đinh Trọng Thắng

Vocals, Guitar

The poetic soul of Ngọt, Vũ's lyrics capture the essence of Vietnamese youth.

Fun Fact: Used a 300k VND guitar to record their first album.

Phan Việt Hoàng

Bass

The backbone of Ngọt's sound, Hoàng brings groove and stability to their music.

Fun Fact: Studied architecture before joining the band.

Nguyễn Hùng Nam Anh

Guitar

Nam Anh's intricate guitar work adds depth and texture to Ngọt's songs.

Fun Fact: Collects vintage guitar pedals.

Trần Bình Tuấn

Drums

Tuấn's dynamic drumming drives Ngọt's energetic performances.

Fun Fact: Started playing drums at age 12.

Ngọt Timeline

2013

Band Formation

Ngọt was formed by Vũ Đinh Trọng Thắng and friends in Hanoi.
